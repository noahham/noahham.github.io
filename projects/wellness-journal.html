Wellness Journal

A survey-based mental journaling app.

This was the product of my first hackathon. When starting to improve your mental health, simply journaling is a hard thing to start doing. It's difficult to know where to start at all.

This app aims to streamline the process a bit better with a survey-based approach. Users are able to pick between short-answer or numerical (rating from 1-10) questions.
