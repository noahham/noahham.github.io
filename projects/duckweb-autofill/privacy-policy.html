DuckWeb Autofill is a Chrome extension designed to help users quickly log in to the University of Oregon’s DuckWeb portal by securely storing and autofilling their credentials.

What We Collect

This extension only collects the following data if you explicitly provide it:

  • DuckWeb username
  • DuckWeb password

This information is stored securely using Chrome’s storage.sync API, which allows credentials to sync across devices using the same Google account. This data is never transmitted to any external servers.

How It's Used

Your credentials are used only to autofill the DuckWeb login form when you visit the site. The extension does not access or interact with any other websites or services.

Security Practices

  • All data is stored using Chrome’s storage.sync, which encrypts data in transit and at rest.
  • Permissions are restricted to the DuckWeb domain only.
  • No tracking, analytics, or third-party scripts are used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to anyone. Ever.

How to Remove Your Data

You can remove your credentials at any time by simply uninstalling the extension. Your data will be deleted automatically.
